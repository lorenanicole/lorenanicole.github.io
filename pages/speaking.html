Speaking

Lorena is passionate about creating opportunities for women in tech as well promoting diversity in tech in general. In her spare time Lorena has been exploring the world of big data and machine learning. An avid learner Lorena speaks about her experience as a beginner to frame seemingly scary or advanced topics in an approachable fashion. She fervently believes that anyone can learn to code, after all everyone was a beginner at some point.
