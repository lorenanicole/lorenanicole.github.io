About

Write/Speak/Code and PyLadies are dedicated to empowering women software developers.

Write/Speak/Code hosts an annual three day conference for women with each day dedicated to writing for technical audiences, learning to speak at conferences, and participating in open source.

PyLadies is an international women Python community that provides mentoring and networking opportunities as well as ongoing educational workshops and talks. PyLadies is proud to be able to offer funding to attend conferences and is sponsored by the Python Software Foundation.
